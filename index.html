About us

MMVM Rice Store is a franchisee of the largest, strongest and most trusted retailer, wholesaler and franchisor of special rice grains in the Philippines, Grainsmart. We take pride in providing the finest selection of premium rice grains in the Philippines. Whether you are a household seeking the perfect staple for your meals or a restaurant in search of top-notch rice supplies, our reliable and reasonably priced offerings are designed to meet your needs.
